# 🪙 Cryptocurrency Market Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of Bitcoin price data (2022-2026) to identify trends,
volatility patterns, and market behavior using Python.

## Key Findings
1. Bitcoin follows a 4-year boom-bust cycle linked to halving
2. 2022 crash: Price dropped from $47K to $16K (crypto winter)
3. 2024-2025 bull run: New all-time high ~$120K driven by ETF approvals
4. Daily volatility (4.26%) is 4x higher than the stock market
5. Volume-Price correlation (0.60) shows moderate relationship
